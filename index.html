The GAMEON Game jam has got to be about connecting with someone, connecting with the World.
Welcome to the rebellion, Player.
GameON is our annual game jam, where participants spend one month creating games based on a theme that we provide. Everyone around the world is welcome to participate, from newbies to professional game developers and your game can be as simple or complex as you want. It’s a great excuse to learn a new gaming blockchain technology, collaborate on something over the weekends with friends, or create a game for the first time!

We’re announcing this year’s theme on Thursday, Dec 31, at 13:37 pm (PDT). From that point, you have 30 days to create a game loosely based on (or inspired by) the theme.

How to participate :

    Create a game based on the theme over the next month. You can participate by yourself or as a team. Multiple submissions are fine. And of course, the use of open source software is encouraged. Engine UNITY3D .

Voting will open shortly after the jam ends and is open to everyone who’s submitted a game. Similar to last year, entries will be rated on the following categories: Overall, Gameplay, Graphics, Audio, Innovation and Theme Interpretation.

There can only be one {Prizes} :

The price of the best GAMEON game.One Amazon giftcard with a value of $ 1000.
